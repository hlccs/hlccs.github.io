Who we are

Heartlake Community Club (HCC) is a team of dedicated students who want to make a difference in their community. We strongly believe in giving back to the community and leading by example. In HCC, we demonstrate these values by running events that contribute to charitable causes while lifitrng school spirit and energy. Some of the events you can be expecting from HCC this year are the Club Fair, ALS Ice Bucket challenge, and a communnity cleanup.
